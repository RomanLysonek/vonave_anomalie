What transferred—and how

Each anomaly idea was adapted to the information available at a historical forecast origin.

DAVID / reconstruction error

Unexpected demand

Deviation from a causal same-weekday expectation provides a local score. A temporal autoencoder supplies a separate systemic reconstruction diagnostic only when V2 artifacts pass provenance checks.

DBAAS / tail calibration

Thresholds from history

Robust residual severity is calibrated with POT/GPD EVT on past validation windows rather than an arbitrary global z-score or a threshold tuned on the target period.

Temporal windows

Origin-known state

Recent anomaly rate and time since the last exceedance can become features because they are known at prediction time. Target-date anomaly status never becomes a predictor.

Detection / action separation

Bounded intervention

Historical scores may attenuate sample weights within fixed floors, while feature and specialist experiments are explicit modes. Rows are never silently deleted.

System perspective

Local versus portfolio shock

Cross-product daily severity identifies broad market movement so plausible retail events are not treated like isolated corrupt observations.

Deployment context

Novel future covariates

Isolation and rarity scores inspect supplied price, discount, campaign and calendar combinations. The path is separate from realised quantities and can run before the forecast week.

What the scores mean

RankingHigher scores mean “more unusual under this causal reference,” creating a review order.
Threshold exceedanceA calibrated operational flag, not proof that the observation is erroneous or harmful.
Known-event overlapExplanatory context that may protect a legitimate peak; never a positive anomaly label.

What cannot be claimed

No detector accuracyWithout adjudicated labels there is no precision, recall, false-positive rate or validated false-alarm claim.
No causal diagnosisThe score identifies unusual association, not the business cause of a demand movement.
No automatic correctionHigh-scoring observations remain data unless a bounded forecast experiment proves value.

The transfer is successful as a falsifiable research layer even though the forecast control remains preferred.
